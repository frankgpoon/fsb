Flashcard Study Buddy

About

Flashcard Study Buddy is an Action on Google powered by Quizlet. Users link their Quizlet accounts and find sets of flash cards using only their voice. Flashcard Study Buddy can retrieve the set and read the cards to users, while waiting for them to answer in order to test them.

To use Flashcard Study Buddy, say "Okay Google, talk to Flashcard Study Buddy" in Google Assistant. If you need help, just say "help" in Flashcard Study Buddy.
